Butterscotch Oatmeal


Description

Morning's inside are always a bore without a good breakfast to start your terrific day. That is why you should crack open the oats, and dig into this devious butterscotch oatmeal delight!


Ingredients

  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Directions

  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, whisk together the egg, milk and brown sugar. Mix in the oats. When the oatmeal begins to boil, cook and stir until thick. Remove from the heat, and stir in butter until melted. Serve immediately.
