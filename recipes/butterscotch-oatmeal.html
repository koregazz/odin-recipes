Butterscotch Oatmeal

Description

Morning's inside are always a bore without a good breakfast to start your terrific day. That is why you should crack open the oats, and dig into this devious butterscotch oatmeal delight!

Ingredients

  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 tablespoons butter
